What's going on here?

The schooling behavior you see is based off of Craig Reynolds' model. The three sliders you see represent the different behavioral abilities of each fish in a school. The separation slider controls the distance that the fish will want to keep from one another. The alignment slider controls how much the fish will want to swim in the same direction. The cohesion slider controls how much a fish wants to stay close to the school.

When a fish flashes white, it means that it's scared. The scare slider controls how comfortable the fish feel around a shark.

Play around witht these sliders and see what types of school behaviors you can create!
